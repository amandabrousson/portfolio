Project Overview

Group project carried out by frontend and backend students. It is a fullstack application designed for purchasing event tickets. Users have the option to log in to buy an event ticket, with the ability to purchase multiple spots per person. They are then redirected to the payment platform, using Stripe. Once the payment is successfully completed, they are redirected back to the website, and the booking details are saved to their profile.
